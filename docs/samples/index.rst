Writing samples
===============

Each sample below follows the same shape: who the reader is, what the piece
had to do, the tools involved, how I worked (including who I would involve on
a real team), and an honest note on what I would improve. The pieces are kept
short on purpose — enough to show the craft without asking a busy reviewer to
read everything.
